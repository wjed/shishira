About Me

The story behind the words

My Journey

I'm a dual-degree student at James Madison University, pursuing both Marketing (BBA) and English Language and Literature (BA), set to graduate in May 2028. This unique combination allows me to bridge the analytical world of business with the creative realm of storytelling.

My academic journey began at Douglas Southall Freeman High School, where I first discovered my passion for writing and communication. Since then, I've been dedicated to honing my craft through both formal education and hands-on experience in various professional settings.

What Drives Me

I believe that effective communication can create positive change in communities. Whether I'm crafting content for social media, developing PR strategies, or contributing to campus publications, I'm driven by the power of authentic storytelling to connect with diverse audiences.

My approach combines strategic thinking with creative expression, always seeking to find the human story within every brand, campaign, or piece of content I create.

Beyond Work

When I'm not writing or strategizing, you'll find me deeply involved in campus life. I'm an active member of Tri Delta, where I serve as Membership Recognition Chair, celebrating the achievements of my sisters and fostering a supportive community.

I also have a passion for music and radio, which led me to my role at WXJM 88.7 FM. There's something magical about college radio that captures the independent spirit I try to bring to all my work. #LONGLIVECOLLEGERADIO!!!!
