Frequently Asked Questions

What types of projects do you take on?

I work on a variety of projects including content creation, marketing strategy, copywriting, and consulting. I'm particularly drawn to projects that allow for authentic storytelling and meaningful connection with audiences.

What's your typical turnaround time?

Turnaround times vary depending on the project scope. For smaller pieces like blog posts or social media content, I typically deliver within 3-5 business days. Larger projects are discussed on a case-by-case basis.

Do you work with students or student organizations?

Absolutely! I love collaborating with fellow students and campus organizations. I offer special rates for student projects and am always excited to support creative endeavors within the university community.

How do you approach new collaborations?

Every collaboration starts with understanding your goals, audience, and unique voice. I believe in transparent communication throughout the process and always aim to exceed expectations while staying true to your brand's authentic story.
