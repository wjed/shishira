About me

I'm a sophomore at James Madison University double-majoring in Marketing (BBA) and English Language and Literature (BA). Most of my days involve writing: opinion columns for The Breeze, feature stories for the JMU College of Integrated Science and Engineering, content for WXJM 88.7's social channels, or a Word doc I'll never show anyone.

I also host a radio show called Death by Chocolate on WXJM 88.7 FM, every Wednesday from 4 to 5:30. It's about the sweet layers of life. I love it more than I can explain.

My hand is permanently scarred where I hold my wooden pencil, and there are fingerprints all over my laptop screen from showing people what I'm working on. I chase the chance to create, in a professional sense and an artistic one. Doing my best.

What I'm doing now

I'm a Feature Writer for the JMU College of Integrated Science and Engineering, where I research and pitch long-form stories about faculty innovation and student research. At The Breeze, I write Culture features and Opinion columns. I run social media for WXJM 88.7 FM and host my own show there. I'm Marketing Chair for the Madison Consulting Club and a Consultant on real client projects.
